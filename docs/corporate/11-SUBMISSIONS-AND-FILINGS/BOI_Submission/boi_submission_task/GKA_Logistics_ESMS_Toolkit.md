### 1. Policy Statement Template

**GKA LOGISTICS ENVIRONMENTAL AND SOCIAL POLICY STATEMENT**

[Company Letterhead]

We, the management of GKA Logistics Services Limited, are committed to conducting our business in a responsible and sustainable manner that protects the environment, ensures the health and safety of our workers, and contributes positively to the communities in which we operate.

**Our Commitments:**

1. **Legal Compliance**: We will comply with all applicable Nigerian laws, regulations, and internationally recognized standards relevant to our operations.

2. **Worker Health and Safety**: We are committed to providing a safe and healthy workplace for all employees, contractors, and visitors. We will prevent work-related injury and ill health by eliminating hazards and minimizing risks.

3. **Environmental Protection**: We will minimize our environmental impact through efficient resource use, pollution prevention, and continuous improvement of our environmental performance.

4. **Community Relations**: We will engage with local communities and other stakeholders in a transparent and respectful manner, addressing concerns and contributing to sustainable development.

5. **Equal Opportunity**: We are committed to providing equal opportunities for all employees and ensuring non-discrimination in all our employment practices.

6. **Continuous Improvement**: We will continually improve our environmental and social performance through regular monitoring, review, and enhancement of our management system.

7. **Supply Chain Responsibility**: We will work with our suppliers and contractors to ensure they meet our environmental and social standards.

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and personnel working on behalf of GKA Logistics Services Limited.

## Grievance Mechanism Tools

### 12. Grievance Procedure

**GKA LOGISTICS GRIEVANCE PROCEDURE**

**Purpose**: To provide a fair and transparent process for employees and external stakeholders to raise concerns, complaints, or suggestions.

**Scope**: Applies to all employees, contractors, and external stakeholders.

**Procedure**:

1. **Raising a Grievance**:
   - Grievances can be raised through:
     - Direct supervisor
     - HR Department
     - HSE Manager
     - Anonymous suggestion box
     - Email to designated address
     - Phone hotline
   - Grievances should be raised as soon as possible after the issue occurs.

2. **Recording the Grievance**:
   - All grievances will be recorded in the Grievance Log
   - Initial acknowledgment will be provided within 24 hours

3. **Initial Assessment**:
   - Simple issues will be addressed within 3 working days
   - Complex issues will be acknowledged within 3 working days with timeline for resolution

4. **Investigation**:
   - Appropriate investigation will be conducted
   - All relevant parties will be consulted
   - Confidentiality will be maintained where possible

5. **Resolution**:
   - Decision will be communicated to the complainant
   - Appropriate corrective actions will be implemented
   - Follow-up will be conducted to ensure resolution

6. **Appeal**:
   - If the complainant is not satisfied with the resolution, they may appeal to the next level of management

**Timeline**:
- Acknowledgment: Within 24 hours
- Simple issues: Within 3 working days
- Complex issues: Within 10 working days
- Appeals: Within 5 working days

**Confidentiality**: All grievances will be handled with appropriate confidentiality.

**No Retaliation**: No employee will face retaliation for raising a genuine grievance.
