## GK and A Logistics Services Ltd Environmental and Social Policy Statement

**Issued by**: GK and A Logistics Services Ltd  
**Date**: October 2025  
**Next Review Date**: October 2026  

We, the management of GK and A Logistics Services Ltd, are committed to conducting our business in a responsible and sustainable manner that protects the environment, ensures the health and safety of our workers, and contributes positively to the communities in which we operate.

### Our Commitments:

1. **Legal Compliance**: We will comply with all applicable Nigerian laws, regulations, and internationally recognized standards relevant to our operations.

2. **Worker Health and Safety**: We are committed to providing a safe and healthy workplace for all employees, contractors, and visitors. We will prevent work-related injury and ill health by eliminating hazards and minimizing risks.

3. **Environmental Protection**: We will minimize our environmental impact through efficient resource use, pollution prevention, and continuous improvement of our environmental performance.

4. **Community Relations**: We will engage with local communities and other stakeholders in a transparent and respectful manner, addressing concerns and contributing to sustainable development.

5. **Equal Opportunity**: We are committed to providing equal opportunities for all employees and ensuring non-discrimination in all our employment practices.

6. **Continuous Improvement**: We will continually improve our environmental and social performance through regular monitoring, review, and enhancement of our management system.

7. **Supply Chain Responsibility**: We will work with our suppliers and contractors to ensure they meet our environmental and social standards.

8. **Climate Resilience**: We will integrate climate change considerations into our planning and operations to build resilience against climate-related risks.

9. **Gender and Social Inclusion**: We are committed to promoting gender equality and social inclusion in our workplace and community engagement activities.

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and personnel working on behalf of GK and A Logistics Services Ltd.

## Senior Management Commitment Statement

**GK and A Logistics Services Ltd Senior Management Commitment to ESMS**

As senior management of GK and A Logistics Services Ltd, we recognize that environmental and social responsibility is essential to our long-term success and sustainability. We are committed to:

1. Providing leadership and resources necessary for the development, implementation, and maintenance of our Environmental and Social Management System.

2. Ensuring that environmental and social considerations are integrated into our business planning and decision-making processes.

3. Promoting awareness throughout the organization of the importance of environmental and social performance.

4. Ensuring compliance with applicable laws, regulations, and other requirements.

5. Setting and reviewing environmental and social objectives and targets.

6. Encouraging continuous improvement in our environmental and social performance.

7. Supporting open communication on environmental and social matters with our stakeholders.

8. Leading by example in our personal conduct and business practices.

We understand that our commitment must be demonstrated through our actions, resource allocation, and decisions. We will regularly review our performance and communicate our progress to our stakeholders.

## Implementation Commitment

We are committed to implementing this policy through:

1. **Resource Allocation**: Providing adequate financial, human, and technical resources for ESMS implementation.

2. **Training and Capacity Building**: Ensuring all employees receive appropriate training on environmental and social management.

3. **Performance Monitoring**: Establishing key performance indicators to track our progress.

4. **Regular Review**: Conducting annual management reviews to assess policy effectiveness.

5. **Stakeholder Engagement**: Maintaining open communication with all stakeholders.

6. **Continuous Improvement**: Regularly updating our practices based on lessons learned and best practices.

This policy statement represents our unwavering commitment to responsible business practices and sustainable development in Nigeria.
