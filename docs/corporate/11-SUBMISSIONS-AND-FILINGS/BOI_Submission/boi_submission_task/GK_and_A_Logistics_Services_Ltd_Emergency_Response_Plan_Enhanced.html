Emergency Response Plan


Executive Statement



Safety, environmental protection, and legal compliance take precedence over production. This Plan is compulsory. Any deviation without prior written approval from the NPA Safety Director and the GK & A Managing Director is a breach subject to disciplinary action, removal from site, and contract termination for cause.


---


Introduction


This Plan is a jointly endorsed, auditable operational control developed under Nigerian Ports Authority (NPA) oversight. Procedures, notification chains, and role interfaces have been reviewed with NPA and are mandatory for all personnel and contractors on GK & A-controlled areas of the NPA Lighter Terminal, Ikorodu.


Aspect Description
**Enforcement** Roles and accountabilities are assigned in writing. Compliance is continuously monitored. Violations are escalated through disciplinary protocols and may trigger regulatory reporting to NPA and relevant authorities.
**Joint Execution** GK & A and NPA Safety Officers shall co-lead emergency activations, joint drills, audits, debriefs, and corrective actions. NPA retains authority to intervene and direct operations where necessary to protect life, environment, and port security.

---


1. Purpose


This Plan establishes an integrated, zero-harm emergency management framework to:


Objective Description
Protection Protect people, property, cargo integrity, and the environment
Prevention Prevent escalation and minimize business interruption
Compliance Achieve and evidence compliance with NPA directives, ISPS Code, IMDG Code, ISO 45001 and ISO 14001 principles, ILO/IMO Code of Practice for Safety and Health in Ports, Lagos State and Federal environmental obligations
Standards Apply the most stringent requirement when standards overlap

Measurable Objectives


Metric Target
Fatalities Zero
Serious Injuries Zero (life-altering)
Notification Time Initiated within 5 minutes of emergency declaration
Headcount Reconciliation Full headcount within 10 minutes of evacuation order
Drill Participation ≥ 90% of on-duty personnel
Corrective Action Closure ≤ 30 days
Spill Containment No uncontrolled discharge to water; all spills contained within site boundary

---


2. Scope


Applies 24/7 to GK & A-controlled operations within the NPA Lighter Terminal, Ikorodu:


Area Description
Primary Operations Quay, jetties, cargo yards, and operational zones under NPA regulation
Storage Warehouses and open storage areas
Refrigeration Reefer container stacks, plug-in racks, and associated power/refrigeration systems

Interfaces and Inclusions


Group Coverage
Personnel All employees, contractors, subcontractors, visitors, clients, and third-party carriers
Third Parties Tenants or third parties while in GK & A-controlled zones
Regulatory Oversight NPA Safety Officers participate in activations, drills, debriefs, and reviews

Exclusions


Category Details
Non-GK & A Premises Excludes non-GK & A premises beyond defined interface points
Mutual Aid GK & A shall support NPA-directed mutual aid and information sharing as requested

3. Definitions


Term Definition
**Emergency** Unplanned event threatening life, environment, assets, cargo, or operations
**Incident Commander (IC)** On-scene authority responsible for declaring and managing the incident; approves re-entry
**Deputy IC** Authorized alternate who assumes command if needed
**Emergency Response Team (ERT)** Trained responders for Fire, HAZMAT/Spill, Medical, Refrigerant/Technical, Security
**Public Information Officer (PIO)** Sole authorized spokesperson for external communications
**Muster Point** Designated safe assembly location for headcount after evacuation
**Exclusion Zone** Controlled perimeter restricting access to trained, authorized personnel only
**ALARP** As Low As Reasonably Practicable; risk threshold for operations and rescue
**Refrigerant** Substances used in reefers (e.g., A1: R-134a, R-404A, R-452A; A2L where applicable)
**Shelter-in-place** Controlled indoor protection procedure during external airborne or security threats
**Command Post (CP)** Primary/secondary locations where IC and key staff coordinate response
**Staging Area** Controlled location where resources and teams assemble before tasking
