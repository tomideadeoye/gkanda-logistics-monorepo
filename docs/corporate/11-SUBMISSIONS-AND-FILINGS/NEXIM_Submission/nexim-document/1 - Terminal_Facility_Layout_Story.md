## Key Facility Components

### 1. The Quay/Wharf: Our Maritime Gateway

At the heart of our operations is our substantial quay facility:

- **Quay Apron Area**: 37,305.84 m²
- **Quay Length**: 1,232.2 m
- **Quay Width**: 28.38 m

This expansive waterfront infrastructure provides ample space for multiple vessel berthing and efficient cargo handling operations. The substantial length allows for simultaneous operations with several vessels, while the width accommodates various cargo types and handling equipment.

### 2. Warehouse Facilities: Secure Storage Solutions

Our facility includes dedicated warehouse space for cargo storage:

- **Warehouse Space**: 6,040.83 m²
- **Paved Stacking Area**: 8,871.10 m²

These areas provide secure, weather-protected storage for goods awaiting distribution or collection, as well as open-air stacking areas for larger cargo items.

---

## Infrastructure Development Story

### Phase 1: Site Preparation and Foundation

Our development journey began with careful site preparation, ensuring the ground could support the heavy loads associated with maritime cargo operations. This involved:

- Geotechnical surveys to assess soil conditions
- Site clearing and grading
- Installation of proper drainage systems

### Phase 2: Quay Construction

The construction of our quay facility was a significant undertaking:

- Deep foundation work to support heavy loads
- Installation of reinforced concrete structures
- Implementation of marine-grade materials to withstand saltwater exposure

### Phase 3: Warehouse and Storage Development

With our maritime gateway established, we developed our storage facilities:

- Construction of climate-controlled warehouse spaces
- Development of paved stacking areas for outdoor storage
- Installation of security systems and lighting

### Phase 4: Security Infrastructure

Recognizing the importance of security in logistics operations, we implemented a comprehensive perimeter security system:

- **Perimeter Fence**: 784.85 m of 2.44 m high galvanized mesh fencing
- **Security Gates**: 
  - Vehicular gates (G1a, G1b, G2, G3-G6) for container and vehicle access
  - Pedestrian gates (P1, P2, P3) for personnel access control
- **Barbed Wire Topping**: Three-strand barbed wire for additional security

## Operational Capabilities

### Cargo Handling Capacity

With our extensive quay length of 1,232.2 meters, we can accommodate multiple vessels simultaneously, significantly increasing our throughput capacity compared to smaller facilities.

### Storage Flexibility

Our combination of warehouse space (6,040.83 m²) and paved stacking area (8,871.10 m²) allows us to handle diverse cargo types:

- Containerized goods in our warehouse facilities
- Bulk cargo in our open stacking areas
- Oversized equipment requiring specialized handling

### Security and Compliance

Our facility meets international standards for security and operations:
