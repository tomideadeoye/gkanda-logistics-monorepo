# GK & A Logistics Services Ltd - Port Terminal Presentation

## For: Nigerian Export-Import Bank (NEXIM)

### Date: October 3, 2025

---

## Project Overview

GK & A Logistics Services Ltd presents our completed port terminal facility at the NPA Lighter Terminal, Ikorodu. This presentation showcases our infrastructure development and key facility details for your consideration.

---

## Terminal Facility Details

### Quay/Wharf Specifications

- **Quay Apron Area**: 37,305.84 m²
- **Quay Length**: 1,232.2 m
- **Quay Width**: 28.38 m

This expansive waterfront infrastructure provides ample space for multiple vessel berthing and efficient cargo handling operations. The substantial length allows for simultaneous operations with several vessels, while the width accommodates various cargo types and handling equipment.

#### Construction Details
- Deep foundation work to support heavy loads
- Installation of reinforced concrete structures
- Implementation of marine-grade materials to withstand saltwater exposure

#### Operational Capacity
With our extensive quay length of 1,232.2 meters, we can accommodate multiple vessels simultaneously, significantly increasing our throughput capacity compared to smaller facilities.

---

## Warehouse and Storage Facilities

Our facility includes dedicated warehouse space for cargo storage and handling:

### Storage Specifications
- **Warehouse Space**: 6,040.83 m²
- **Paved Stacking Area**: 8,871.10 m²

These areas provide secure, weather-protected storage for goods awaiting distribution or collection, as well as open-air stacking areas for larger cargo items. Our combination of warehouse space and paved stacking area allows us to handle diverse cargo types including containerized goods, bulk cargo, and oversized equipment requiring specialized handling.

## Completed Security Infrastructure

As part of our commitment to providing a secure and compliant facility, we have completed the perimeter security fence installation around the terminal.

### Fence Installation Details

- **Total Fence Length**: Approximately 784.85 m
- **Fence Height**: 2.44 m with three-strand barbed-wire topping
- **Posts**: 288 total posts (SHS 60x60x3.0 mm S355 line posts)
- **Mesh**: 50 mm aperture galvanized mesh, 3.55 mm wire thickness

## Gate System

The terminal features a comprehensive gate system for vehicle and pedestrian access control:

### Vehicular Gates
- **G1a & G1b**: Sliding gates, 5.72 m each (inbound/outbound)
- **G2**: Container gate, 12.19 m (sliding)
- **G3-G6**: Standard gates, 6.10 m each (sliding)

### Pedestrian Gates
- **P1**: Turnstile gate
- **P2**: Inter-terminal access gate
- **P3**: Emergency exit gate

## Technical Compliance

Our installation meets all required standards and regulations:

- **Nigerian Building Code** compliance
- **BS EN 1991-1-4**: Wind loading standards
- **BS EN 1993**: Steel structure design
- **BS EN 1992**: Concrete structure design
- **BS 1722**: Chain-link fencing standards
- **ISO 1461**: Hot-dip galvanizing specifications
- **ISO 12944**: Protective paint systems (Category C5-M marine exposure)

---

## Materials and Durability

### Structural Components
- **Steel Posts**: S355 grade steel (SHS 60x60x3.0 mm)
- **End/Corner Posts**: Upgraded SHS 80x80x4.0 mm sections
- **Mesh**: 50 mm aperture galvanized wire (3.55 mm thickness)

### Corrosion Protection
- **Duplex Coating System**: Hot-dip galvanizing plus epoxy/polyurethane topcoats
- **Total DFT**: 160-200 um for marine exposure (C5-M category)
- **Concrete**: C25/30 grade with XS3/XD3 exposure classes

---

## Quality Assurance

Our installation has undergone rigorous quality control processes:

- **Concrete Testing**: 7/28-day cube tests confirming C25/30 specification
- **Coating Inspection**: DFT measurements verifying coating thickness
- **Galvanizing Certification**: Thickness checks confirming ISO 1461 compliance
- **Bolt Torque Verification**: Torque checks on critical fixings
- **Anchor Testing**: Pull tests on foundation anchors where applicable

---

## Maintenance and Operations

We provide a comprehensive O&M plan for long-term facility sustainability:

- **Monthly Inspections**: Visual checks of mesh tension, fixings, and corrosion
- **Quarterly Maintenance**: Lubrication of sliding gate components
- **Six-Monthly Checks**: Torque verification of bolts and fixings
- **Annual Services**: Coating touch-ups and earthing system testing
