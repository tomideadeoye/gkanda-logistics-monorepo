🏢 About Us
GK and A Logistics Services Ltd is a Nigerian-based maritime infrastructure and logistics development company committed to reshaping how goods move across the country.
Our mission is to provide world-class logistics solutions through the design, development, and operation of inland ports and related infrastructure that are efficient, sustainable, and economically impactful.
We are currently delivering two key projects that position us at the forefront of Nigeria’s inland port evolution—both located in the strategically significant Ikorodu region of Lagos.

📦 PROJECT ONE
20,000 SQM NPA Lighter Terminal, Ikorodu
Poised for Immediate Operations
GK&A has secured a 20,000 square meter lease from the Nigerian Ports Authority (NPA) within the Lighter Terminal at Ikorodu. The project will begin operations in the coming weeks and will serve as a logistics hub to handle immediate demand and support the upcoming regional port development.
Key Facilities:
	•	🏗 EPT Terminal for Empty Container Processing
	•	🚛 Holding Bay for Truck Staging and Cargo Movement
	•	📦 Logistics Services including Cargo Handling & Temporary Storage
	•	🔁 Operational integration with ongoing port activities
Strategic Objectives:
	•	Reduce congestion and turnaround time in Lagos' port ecosystem
	•	Serve as a transitional platform for future large-scale port activity
	•	Create jobs and logistics stability in the Ikorodu axis
	•	Provide a platform for immediate business operations for private partners

⚓ PROJECT TWO
Ikorodu Regional Inland Port
150 Hectares Underway – Expandable to 667 Hectares
Located adjacent to the NPA Lighter Terminal, this project represents a bold vision for Nigeria’s next major port hub. We are developing a Regional Inland Port on 150 hectares of land (with an expansion potential of up to 667 hectares), offering a long-term solution to the congestion issues at Apapa and Tin Can Island ports.
Current Status:
	•	Environmental & regulatory approvals secured
	•	Reclamation of the 150 hectares has commenced
	•	Approved for expansion up to 667 hectares
	•	Government support and technical groundwork in place
Port Vision Includes:
	•	🛳 Full Port Terminal Infrastructure (cargo, container, RoRo)
	•	🏭 Industrial Cluster Zones for logistics, finance, and innovation
	•	📦 Warehousing and Value-Added Services
	•	💡 Digital Innovation & Equipment Manufacturing Hubs
Strategic Impact:
	•	Decongest Lagos' current port bottlenecks
	•	Catalyze regional commerce and industrialization
	•	Attract long-term investments and PPP partnerships
