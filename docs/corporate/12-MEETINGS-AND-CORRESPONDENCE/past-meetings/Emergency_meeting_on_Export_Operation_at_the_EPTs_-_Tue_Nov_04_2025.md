### **Emergency Meeting on Export Operation at the EPTs - Tue Nov 04, 2025**

## TLDR (Too Long; Didn't Read)

This emergency meeting addressed severe congestion at the port corridors, which is undermining the entire export process. The primary cause was identified as Export Processing Terminals (EPTs) sending unmanifested trucks, unrequested trucks, or trucks arriving days after their manifest slot has expired. Management has taken a zero-tolerance stance, issuing strict directives and warning that non-compliant EPTs will face serious sanctions, including suspension and delistment from the program, in order to restore order and efficiency.

## Action Items

Here are the specific directives and tasks assigned during the meeting:

### For All EPTs:

* **Revisit the SOP**: Immediately review the official Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for Export Processing Terminals.
* **Cease Illicit Bookings**: Stop the practice of booking trucks for export that are not physically processed at your designated terminal. All exports must originate from the booking EPT.
* **Adhere to 24-Hour Manifest Validity**: Strictly enforce the 24-hour validity period for all manifests. Trucks arriving after this window will be turned back.
* **No Unofficial Annexes**: EPTs located far from the port must apply for and receive official NPA approval for a closer annex or staging area. Using unapproved locations is forbidden.
* **Fix Functional Barriers**: Report and repair any non-functional barriers immediately. The use of handheld devices is a temporary fix, not a long-term solution.

### For the Export Desk (led by Mrs. Silori):

* **Distribute the SOP**: Send a digital copy of the official EPT SOP to all terminal operators to ensure everyone has the correct and updated version.
* **Conduct Unscheduled Audits**: The Export Desk is now mandated to perform unscheduled, surprise visits to all EPTs to verify their operations and ensure compliance with the SOP.

### For TTP (Truck Transit Park):

* **Report on Barrier Status**: Provide a comprehensive report to management detailing which EPTs are using handheld devices due to non-functional barriers and for how long.
* **Enforce Barrier Repairs**: Stop allowing handhelds as a permanent substitute for barriers and work with EPTs to ensure repairs are made promptly.

### For Enforcement & Traffic Managers (TMs):

* **Strictly Enforce Manifest Rules**: Enforce the 24-hour manifest validity rule at all port entry points. Refuse entry to any truck arriving outside its designated window or without proper manifesting.
