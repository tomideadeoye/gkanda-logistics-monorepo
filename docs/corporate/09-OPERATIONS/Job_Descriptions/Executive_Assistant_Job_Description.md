# Executive Assistant to the Managing Director

## GK and A Logistics Services Ltd

### Job Title: Executive Assistant to the Managing Director

### Reports to: Omobola Abiru (Managing Director)

### Department: Executive Office

### Location: NPA Lighter Terminal, Ikorodu, Lagos, Nigeria

---

## Job Summary

The Executive Assistant to the Managing Director provides high-level administrative support to the Managing Director while taking on significant operational responsibilities in contract management, business development, and regulatory compliance. This role serves as a bridge between executive leadership and operational teams, ensuring smooth execution of strategic initiatives and day-to-day business operations.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Executive Support
- Provide comprehensive administrative support to the Managing Director
- Manage executive calendar, appointments, and travel arrangements
- Prepare and organize materials for meetings, presentations, and board sessions
- Handle confidential correspondence and communications
- Act as primary point of contact for executive-level inquiries
- Coordinate between the Managing Director and internal/external stakeholders

### 2. Contract Management
- Oversee contracts between GK and A Logistics Services Ltd and various service providers
- Monitor contract performance and compliance
- Coordinate contract renewals, amendments, and terminations
- Maintain comprehensive contract database and documentation
- Ensure timely execution of contractual obligations
- Manage vendor relationships and performance evaluations

### 3. Import/Export Services Coordination
- Identify and coordinate import/export service requirements
- Liaise with shipping lines, customs brokers, and freight forwarders
- Monitor international trade regulations and compliance requirements
- Coordinate customs clearance and documentation processes
- Manage relationships with import/export service providers
- Ensure smooth execution of international logistics operations

### 4. Business Development
- Identify potential business opportunities in maritime logistics sector
- Research and qualify prospective clients and partners
- Support development of strategic partnerships and alliances
- Assist in market analysis and competitive intelligence gathering
- Participate in industry networking and relationship building
- Contribute to business expansion initiatives

### 5. Proposal Development
- Draft comprehensive proposals for regulatory submissions and approvals
- Create detailed proposals for clients requiring maritime logistics services
- Develop technical and commercial proposals for potential partners
- Ensure proposals meet regulatory requirements and company standards
- Coordinate proposal reviews and approvals
- Track proposal outcomes and follow-up activities

### 6. Project Coordination
- Manage multi-stakeholder projects from initiation to completion
- Coordinate site plan submissions and regulatory approvals
- Coordinate infrastructure installation projects
- Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements
- Prepare and submit documentation packages
- Maintain organized documentation repositories

### 7. Operational Support
- Assist in daily operations management and problem-solving
- Coordinate between different departments and external parties
- Monitor project timelines and deliverable deadlines
- Provide administrative support for operational initiatives
- Assist in budget tracking and expense management
- Support quality assurance and compliance activities

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Contract Management
- High rate of on-time contract renewals and reviews
- Minimal contract compliance issues
- Positive vendor relationships

### Business Development
- Regular identification of qualified business opportunities
- Successful conversion of leads
- Development of strategic partnerships

### Project Execution
- Consistent on-time delivery of assigned projects
- Accurate regulatory submissions
- Successful completion of tasks within reasonable deadlines

### Operational Support
- Prompt response to executive inquiries
- Accurate documentation and reporting
- Positive feedback from internal stakeholders
