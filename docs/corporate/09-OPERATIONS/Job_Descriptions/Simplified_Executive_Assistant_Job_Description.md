# Executive Assistant to the Managing Director

## GK and A Logistics Services Ltd

### Job Title: Executive Assistant to the Managing Director

### Reports to: Managing Director

### Location: NPA Lighter Terminal, Ikorodu, Lagos, Nigeria

---

## Job Summary

The Executive Assistant to the Managing Director provides high-level administrative support while taking on significant operational responsibilities in contract management, business development, and regulatory compliance. This role serves as a critical bridge between executive leadership and operational teams.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### Executive Support
- Manage executive calendar, appointments, and travel arrangements
- Prepare materials for meetings and presentations
- Handle confidential correspondence and communications
- Coordinate between the Managing Director and stakeholders

### Contract Management
- Oversee contracts with service providers
- Monitor contract performance and compliance
- Manage contract renewals and documentation
- Maintain contract database

### Import/Export Services Coordination
- Coordinate import/export service requirements
- Liaise with shipping lines and customs brokers
- Monitor international trade regulations
- Manage relationships with service providers

### Business Development
- Identify potential business opportunities
- Research and qualify prospective clients
- Support development of strategic partnerships
- Participate in industry networking

### Proposal Development
- Draft proposals for regulatory submissions
- Create client service proposals
- Ensure proposals meet requirements
- Coordinate proposal reviews

### Project Coordination
- Manage multi-stakeholder projects
- Coordinate site plan submissions
- Oversee technical drawings review
- Ensure regulatory compliance

### Documentation Management
- Prepare documentation packages for regulatory bodies
- Convert technical drawings from SVG to PDF
- Maintain organized documentation repositories
- Ensure document version control

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Contract Management
- 100% on-time contract renewals
- Zero compliance violations

### Business Development
- Monthly identification of qualified opportunities
- Successful lead conversion

### Project Execution
- 95% on-time project delivery
- 100% accuracy in submissions

### Operational Support
- Prompt response to executive inquiries
- High accuracy in documentation
