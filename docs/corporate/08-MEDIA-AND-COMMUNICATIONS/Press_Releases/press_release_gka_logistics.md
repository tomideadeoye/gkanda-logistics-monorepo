IKORODU, Nigeria, October 22, 2025 — GK AND A LOGISTICS SERVICES LTD ANNOUNCES OPERATIONAL STATUS OF IKORODU EXPORT PROCESSING TERMINAL

## PRESS RELEASE

GK and A Logistics Services Ltd today announced that its export processing terminal located within the Nigerian Ports Authority (NPA) Lighter Terminal in Ikorodu, Lagos State is now fully operational and open for business.

This development aligns with the agenda of President Muhammadu Buhari's administration to diversify the national economy and reduce dependence on oil exports through enhanced non-oil trade facilitation. The facility represents a significant step forward in GK and A Logistics Services Ltd's mission to reshape how goods move across Nigeria through world-class logistics solutions and innovative maritime infrastructure.

The operational facility spans 20,000 square meters and serves as a crucial gateway for Nigeria's import and export activities. The terminal provides comprehensive logistics services including empty container processing, truck staging, cargo handling, and temporary storage.

The Ikorodu facility represents the first phase of GK and A Logistics Services Ltd's comprehensive two-project development strategy. The company is simultaneously developing the Ikorodu Regional Inland Port on 150 hectares of land, with expansion potential up to 667 hectares. This future-ready port hub will feature full terminal infrastructure, industrial cluster zones, and digital innovation services.

Key operational features include:
- Empty Container Processing Terminal (EPT) for specialized container management
- Holding Bay for efficient truck staging and cargo movement coordination
- Comprehensive cargo handling and climate-controlled temporary storage
- Integrated port operations that complement existing NPA facilities

What sets GK and A Logistics Services Ltd apart is its unique position as both an immediate operational partner and a long-term infrastructure innovator. The company offers customers both immediate solutions and future-ready partnerships through its strategic location that provides direct access to maritime shipping routes and connectivity to Lagos' extensive road network.

GK and A Logistics Services Ltd has secured all necessary environmental and regulatory approvals for both projects, with reclamation of the 150-hectare site for the Regional Inland Port already underway. The company maintains strong institutional alignment with NPA, NIWA, and other key agencies, ensuring smooth project execution and regulatory compliance.

The company's vision extends beyond traditional port operations to include:
- Full Port Terminal Infrastructure (cargo, container, RoRo)
- Industrial Cluster Zones for logistics, finance, and innovation
- Warehousing and Value-Added Services
- Digital Innovation & Equipment Manufacturing Hubs

GK and A Logistics Services Ltd is actively seeking partnerships with port operators, shipping lines, freight forwarders, and businesses engaged in import/export activities. The company welcomes both operational collaborations and strategic investment opportunities as it continues to build Nigeria's future in inland ports and logistics.
