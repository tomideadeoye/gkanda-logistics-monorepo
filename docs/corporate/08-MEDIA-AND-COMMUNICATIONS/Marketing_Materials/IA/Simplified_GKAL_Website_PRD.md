# GK and A Logistics Services Ltd Website - Simplified Product Requirements Document

## Executive Summary

GK and A Logistics Services Ltd needs a modern website to showcase services and provide essential customer tools. The website will help establish the company as Nigeria's premier maritime logistics provider.

## Key Objectives
- Showcase terminal capabilities and services
- Provide 24/7 customer access to essential services
- Enable seamless customer interactions
- Position the company as a technology-forward logistics partner

## Main Features Required

### Homepage
- Video background showing terminal operations
- Clear calls-to-action: "Get Quote", "Track Cargo", "Contact Us"
- Customer logos showcase
- Services overview

### Core Functionality

#### Container Tracking
- Search by container ID, booking ID, or bill of lading
- Save containers to watchlist (for registered users)
- Email notifications for status updates

#### Vessel Schedule
- Show arrival/departure times for vessels
- Filter by location and date
- Focus on Nigeria/Onne region

#### Terminal Alerts
- SMS/email notifications for operational updates
- Weather warnings, congestion alerts, customs delays
- Customer subscription management

#### Service Locator
- Interactive map with terminal locations
- Contact information for each location
- Directions to terminals

### Customer Accounts
- Registration and login
- Profile management
- Saved container watchlists
- Alert preferences

### Content Sections
- Services descriptions (easily updatable)
- News and company updates
- Client testimonials
- Contact information

### Essential Pages
- Home
- Services
- Track & Trace
- Locations
- News
- About Us
- Contact

## Technical Requirements
- Modern, responsive design (works on mobile/desktop)
- Fast loading times (<3 seconds)
- Secure user authentication
- Integration with Google Maps
- Content management system for easy updates

## Success Metrics
- Increase in qualified leads
- Improved customer satisfaction
- Reduction in phone inquiries through self-service
- Positive user feedback on digital experience
