# TikTok Setup Guide

## Step-by-Step Setup Instructions

### 1. Account Creation
1. Download the TikTok app from the App Store or Google Play Store
2. Open the app and tap "Sign up"
3. Choose to sign up with phone number, email, or third-party account
4. Enter your information and verify your account
5. Complete your profile setup

### 2. Profile Optimization
1. Tap "Edit profile"
2. Upload a recognizable profile picture (company logo works well)
3. Create a username that matches your brand
4. Write a compelling bio that explains what you do
5. Add a link to your website
6. Include relevant hashtags in your bio

### 3. Account Conversion
1. Go to Settings and Privacy
2. Select "Account"
3. Tap "Switch to Pro Account"
4. Choose "Business"
5. Select your business category

## Detailed Content Requirements

### Profile Picture Guidelines
- Clear company logo or recognizable image
- Square format
- Size: 200x200 pixels minimum
- Format: JPG or PNG

### Bio Template
- Brief company description (200 characters max)
- Value proposition
- Website link
- Relevant hashtags
- Call-to-action

## Content Strategy Recommendations

### Target Audience
- Younger demographics (Gen Z and Millennials)
- Industry professionals
- Potential customers
- Entertainment seekers

### Posting Frequency
- Recommended: 3-5 videos per week
- Consistency is key for algorithm favorability
- Peak posting times: Tuesday-Saturday, 6-10 AM and 7-11 PM

### Content Themes
- Quick tips and tutorials
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Product demonstrations
- Industry trends and commentary
- Challenges and trends
- Employee spotlights
- Customer testimonials
- Day-in-the-life content

### Video Optimization
1. Use trending sounds and music
2. Create eye-catching thumbnails
3. Include captions for accessibility
4. Keep videos short (15-60 seconds)
5. Add relevant hashtags
6. Engage with comments promptly

## Advanced Features

### TikTok LIVE
1. Broadcast live to engage with your audience
2. Great for Q&A sessions, product launches, and events
3. Promote in advance for maximum attendance
4. Interact with viewers in real-time

### TikTok Duets and Stitches
1. Collaborate with other creators
2. Respond to trending content
3. Showcase your products in action
4. Increase reach through collaboration

### TikTok Branded Effects
1. Create custom filters and effects
2. Increase brand recognition
3. Encourage user-generated content
4. Requires TikTok Ads Manager account

### TikTok Analytics
1. Access through Pro Account dashboard
2. Monitor:
   - Video views and engagement
   - Follower growth
   - Audience demographics
   - Popular content

## Advertising Options

### TikTok Ads
1. In-feed ads
2. Branded hashtag challenges
3. Branded effects
4. TopView (premium placement)
