## Executive Summary

This guide consolidates all essential features for the GK and A Logistics Services Ltd website, focusing exclusively on capabilities that directly serve GK&A's operations at NPA Lighter Terminal, Ikorodu. All global maritime service locator features have been removed as they are not relevant to GK&A's single-terminal operation.

**Key Focus Areas:**
- Customer self-service tools for cargo tracking
- Professional presentation of GK&A's terminal capabilities
- Lead generation through sales contact integration
- Operational transparency through vessel schedules and alerts

## Core Website Features

### Primary Purpose
The GK and A Logistics Services Ltd website serves as a digital gateway for B2B customers, providing:
- Real-time cargo tracking capabilities
- Professional showcase of terminal operations
- Direct communication channels for sales and support
- Operational transparency through schedules and alerts

### Target Users
- **Import/Export Managers**: Need reliable tracking and communication
- **Logistics Coordinators**: Require operational visibility and support
- **Shipping Company Representatives**: Seek schedule information and terminal updates
- **Potential Clients**: Research GK&A's capabilities and services
