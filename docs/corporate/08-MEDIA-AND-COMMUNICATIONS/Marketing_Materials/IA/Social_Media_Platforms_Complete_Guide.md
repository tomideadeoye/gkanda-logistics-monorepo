## LinkedIn Setup Guide

### Step-by-Step Setup Instructions

#### 1. Account Creation
1. Go to [LinkedIn.com](https://www.linkedin.com/)
2. Click "Join now"
3. Enter your email, password, first name, and last name
4. Verify your email address through the confirmation email sent by LinkedIn
5. **Alternatively, if setting up a Company Page:**
   - Click "Work" in the navigation bar
   - Select "Create a Company Page"

#### 2. Profile Completion
1. Navigate to your profile by clicking your profile picture
2. Click the pencil/edit icon under your profile picture
3. Upload a professional profile picture (recommended size: 400x400 pixels)
4. Add a background/banner image (recommended size: 1584x396 pixels)
5. Complete the "About" section with a compelling company description
6. Add your website URL in the contact info section
7. Include relevant hashtags for discoverability

#### 3. Company Page Specific Setup (if applicable)
1. Add your company logo
2. Complete all basic information fields (industry, company size, etc.)
3. Add specialties that relate to your business
4. Include your company's physical address if applicable
5. Add any relevant showcase pages for different divisions

### Content Requirements

#### Profile Picture Guidelines
- Professional headshot or company logo
- Clear, high-quality image
- Proper lighting and background
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: 400x400 pixels

#### Banner Image Guidelines
- Professional and branded
- Include tagline or key services
- Size: 1584x396 pixels
- Format: JPG or PNG

#### Company Description Template
- Opening hook to grab attention
- Brief company overview (what you do)
- Key value propositions
- Call-to-action for connection
- Relevant hashtags

### Content Strategy

#### Target Audience
- Industry professionals
- Potential clients/customers
- Partners and suppliers
- Job seekers (if hiring)
- Industry influencers

#### Posting Frequency
- Recommended: 3-5 posts per week
- Peak engagement times: Tuesday-Thursday, 7-9 AM and 12-3 PM

#### Content Themes
- Industry insights and news
- Company milestones and achievements
- Employee spotlights
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Educational content
- Client success stories
- Relevant industry events

### Advanced Features

#### LinkedIn Articles
1. Click the "Start a post" button
2. Select "Write article"
3. Craft engaging headlines
4. Use subheadings and bullet points for readability
5. Include relevant images and data visualizations

#### LinkedIn Live
1. Available only for established pages
2. Requires approval from LinkedIn
3. Great for Q&A sessions, behind-the-scenes content, and events

#### LinkedIn Polls
1. Click the poll icon when creating a post
2. Create engaging questions for your audience
3. Use for market research and engagement

### Analytics and Monitoring

#### LinkedIn Analytics Dashboard
1. Access through your company page
2. Monitor:
   - Follower demographics
   - Post engagement rates
   - Visitor demographics
   - Update performance

## Facebook Business Page Setup

### Step-by-Step Setup Instructions

#### 1. Page Creation
1. Go to [facebook.com/pages/create](https://www.facebook.com/pages/create)
2. Select "Business or Brand"
3. Enter your company name
4. Add category that best describes your business
5. Add your company's address if applicable
6. Click "Create Page"

#### 2. Page Optimization
1. Click "Add Profile and Cover Photo"
2. Upload a professional profile picture (recommended: company logo)
3. Upload a cover photo that represents your brand
4. Complete all basic information in the "About" section:
   - Category
   - Address
   - Phone number
   - Website
   - Hours (if applicable)
   - Additional contact information

#### 3. Page Verification
1. Click "Settings" at the top of your page
2. Select "Page Transparency"
3. Click "Verify this Page"
4. Follow the verification process (may require official documentation)

### Content Requirements

#### Profile Picture Guidelines
- Clear company logo
- Square format (recommended: 170x170 pixels)
- Format: JPG or PNG
- High contrast for visibility

#### Cover Photo Guidelines
- Represents your brand and services
- Size: 820x312 pixels
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Include tagline or key services

#### About Section Template
- Brief company description
- Contact information
- Hours of operation
- Website link
- Additional services offered

### Content Strategy

#### Target Audience
- Local community
- Existing customers
- Industry professionals
- Potential clients
- Partners and suppliers

#### Posting Frequency
- Recommended: 1-2 posts per day
- Peak engagement times: Wednesday-Friday, 1-3 PM

#### Content Themes
- Company news and updates

- Customer testimonials

#### Facebook Groups Strategy

### Advanced Features

#### Facebook Events

#### Facebook Live

#### Facebook Ads

## Instagram Setup Guide

### Step-by-Step Setup Instructions

#### 1. Account Creation
1. Download the Instagram app from the App Store or Google Play Store
2. Open the app and tap "Sign up"
3. Choose to sign up with email, phone number, or Facebook account
4. Enter your information and verify your account
5. Set up your profile by adding a username and password
6. **Convert to a Business Account (highly recommended for companies):**
   - Go to Settings
   - Tap "Account"
   - Select "Switch to Professional Account"
   - Choose "Business"
   - Connect to your Facebook Page (create one if needed)

#### 2. Profile Optimization
1. Tap "Edit Profile"
2. Upload a recognizable profile picture (company logo works well)
3. Create a recognizable username (ideally matching your business name)
4. Write a compelling bio that explains what you do
5. Add a link to your website
6. Include a contact method (email, phone number, or address)

#### 3. Content Preparation
1. Plan your visual aesthetic and color scheme
2. Create a content calendar
3. Prepare your first 10-15 posts in advance
4. Set up story highlights for important categories

### Content Requirements

#### Profile Picture Guidelines
- Clear company logo or recognizable image
- Centered and properly framed
- Size: 110x110 pixels minimum
- Format: JPG or PNG

#### Bio Template
- Brief company description (150 characters max)
- Value proposition
- Call-to-action
- Emojis for visual appeal
- Link to website

#### Content Guidelines
- **Recommended aspect ratios:**
  - Square photos: 1:1
  - Portrait photos: 4:5
  - Landscape photos: 1.91:1
  - Stories: 9:16
- **Image size recommendations:**
  - Square: 1080x1080 pixels
  - Portrait: 1080x1350 pixels
  - Landscape: 1080x566 pixels

### Content Strategy

#### Target Audience
- Demographics: Age, gender, location
- Interests: Related industries, hobbies, lifestyle
- Behaviors: Shopping habits, device usage

#### Posting Frequency
- Recommended: 3-7 posts per week
- Stories: Daily (if possible)
- Peak engagement times: Monday-Friday, 11 AM-1 PM

#### Content Themes
- Product showcases
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Customer testimonials
- Industry news and tips
- Team introductions
- Event coverage
- User-generated content

#### Hashtag Strategy
- Mix of popular, niche, and branded hashtags
- Research competitor hashtags
- Use 5-10 relevant hashtags per post
- Create a branded hashtag for your company

### Advanced Features

#### Instagram Stories
1. Share daily behind-the-scenes content
2. Use interactive features:
   - Polls
   - Questions
   - Countdown timers
   - Emoji sliders
3. Create highlight covers for organization

#### Instagram Reels
1. Create short, engaging videos (15-30 seconds)
2. Use trending audio
3. Add captions for accessibility
4. Include a clear call-to-action

#### Instagram Guides
1. Curate collections of posts on specific topics
2. Great for tutorials, product collections, or resources

#### Instagram Shopping
1. Requires a business account
2. Must be approved for shopping
3. Connect a product catalog
4. Tag products in posts and stories

### Analytics and Monitoring

#### Instagram Insights
1. Access through your business account
2. Monitor:
   - Follower demographics
   - Post reach and engagement
   - Story views and interactions
   - Website clicks

## Twitter (X) Setup Guide

### Step-by-Step Setup Instructions

#### 1. Account Creation
1. Go to [twitter.com](https://www.twitter.com/) or download the Twitter app
2. Click "Sign up"
3. Enter your phone number or email address
4. Create a password
5. Verify your account through the confirmation code sent via SMS or email
6. Complete your profile setup

#### 2. Profile Optimization
1. Click on your profile icon and select "Settings and privacy"
2. Click "Profile" to edit your profile information
3. Upload a professional profile picture (company logo works well)
4. Add a header image that represents your brand
5. Complete your bio with a compelling description
6. Add your website URL
7. Include your location if relevant

#### 3. Account Verification
1. For business accounts, consider applying for verification:
   - Go to Settings and privacy
   - Select "Account"
   - Click "Request verification"
   - Submit required documentation

### Content Requirements

#### Profile Picture Guidelines
- Clear company logo or recognizable image
- Square format
- Size: 400x400 pixels minimum
- Format: JPG, PNG, or GIF

#### Header Image Guidelines
- Branded banner that represents your company
- Size: 1500x500 pixels
- Format: JPG, PNG, or GIF

#### Bio Template
- Brief company description (160 characters max)
- Value proposition
- Website link
- Relevant hashtags or mentions
- Emojis for visual appeal

### Content Strategy

#### Target Audience
- Industry professionals
- Potential customers
- Partners and suppliers
- Influencers in your field
- Media and journalists

#### Posting Frequency
- Recommended: 5-10 tweets per day
- Peak engagement times: Tuesday-Friday, 9 AM-3 PM

#### Content Themes
- Industry news and insights

#### Hashtag Strategy

### Advanced Features

#### Twitter Spaces
1. Host live audio conversations

#### Twitter Lists
1. Create lists to organize accounts you follow
2. Create public lists for industry leaders
3. Use lists to monitor competitors

#### Twitter Moments
1. Curate collections of tweets around events or topics
2. Great for showcasing company milestones or events

#### Twitter Ads
1. Promote tweets to reach a larger audience
2. Create promoted accounts to gain followers
3. Use promoted trends for major campaigns

### Analytics and Monitoring

## General Best Practices

### Brand Consistency
- Use consistent profile pictures across all platforms
- Maintain unified brand voice and messaging
- Coordinate posting schedules
- Cross-promote between platforms
- Follow community engagement protocols

### Content Creation Guidelines
- Create high-quality, valuable content
- Use platform-specific formatting and features
- Include clear calls-to-action
- Engage with your audience regularly
- Monitor and respond to comments/messages

### Timing and Scheduling
- Research optimal posting times for each platform
- Create a content calendar
- Batch content creation for efficiency
- Use scheduling tools for consistency

### Community Management
- Respond to comments and messages within 24 hours
- Engage with other accounts in your industry
- Build relationships with influencers
- Monitor brand mentions and sentiment

## Content Strategy Framework

### Content Pillars
1. **Educational Content**: Industry insights, tips, tutorials
2. **Behind-the-Scenes**: Company culture, team introductions
3. **Product/Service Highlights**: Showcasing offerings
4. **Customer Stories**: Testimonials, case studies
5. **Industry News**: Relevant updates and trends
6. **Engagement Content**: Polls, questions, discussions

### Content Mix Recommendations
- **LinkedIn**: 60% educational, 20% company news, 20% engagement
- **Facebook**: 40% community-focused, 30% promotional, 30% educational
- **Instagram**: 50% visual content, 30% stories, 20% educational
- **Twitter**: 40% industry news, 30% engagement, 30% company updates

### Hashtag Strategy
- Research relevant hashtags for each platform
- Create branded hashtags for campaigns
- Use a mix of popular and niche hashtags
- Monitor hashtag performance and adjust
