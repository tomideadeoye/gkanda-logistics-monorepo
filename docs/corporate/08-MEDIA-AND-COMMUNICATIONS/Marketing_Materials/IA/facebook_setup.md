# Facebook Business Page Setup Guide

## Step-by-Step Setup Instructions

### 1. Page Creation
1. Go to [facebook.com/pages/create](https://www.facebook.com/pages/create)
2. Select "Business or Brand"
3. Enter your company name
4. Add category that best describes your business
5. Add your company's address if applicable
6. Click "Create Page"

### 2. Page Optimization
1. Click "Add Profile and Cover Photo"
2. Upload a professional profile picture (recommended: company logo)
3. Upload a cover photo that represents your brand
4. Complete all basic information in the "About" section:
   - Category
   - Address
   - Phone number
   - Website
   - Hours (if applicable)
   - Additional contact information

### 3. Page Verification
1. Click "Settings" at the top of your page
2. Select "Page Transparency"
3. Click "Verify this Page"
4. Follow the verification process (may require official documentation)

## Detailed Content Requirements

### Profile Picture Guidelines
- Clear company logo
- Square format (recommended: 170x170 pixels)
- Format: JPG or PNG
- High contrast for visibility

### Cover Photo Guidelines
- Represents your brand and services
- Size: 820x312 pixels
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Include tagline or key services

### About Section Template
- Brief company description
- Contact information
- Hours of operation
- Website link
- Additional services offered

## Content Strategy Recommendations

### Target Audience
- Local community
- Existing customers
- Industry professionals
- Potential clients
- Partners and suppliers

### Posting Frequency
- Recommended: 1-2 posts per day
- Peak engagement times: Wednesday-Friday, 1-3 PM
- Mix of promotional, educational, and engaging content

### Content Themes
- Company news and updates
- Product/service highlights
- Customer testimonials
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Industry news and insights
- Community involvement
- Promotional offers
- User-generated content

### Facebook Groups Strategy
1. Create private groups for customers or community
2. Share exclusive content with group members
3. Foster discussion and engagement
4. Provide customer support

## Advanced Features

### Facebook Shops
1. Set up a shop tab on your page
2. Add products to your catalog
3. Enable checkout functionality
4. Promote products through posts

### Facebook Events
1. Create events for company milestones or launches
2. Invite followers and community members
3. Share event updates and behind-the-scenes content
4. Post-event follow-up content

### Facebook Live
1. Broadcast live video to engage with audience
2. Great for product demonstrations, Q&A sessions
3. Promote in advance for maximum attendance
4. Save videos for later viewing

### Facebook Ads
1. Boost posts to reach a larger audience
2. Create targeted ad campaigns
3. Promote page likes and engagement
4. Drive traffic to your website

## Analytics and Monitoring

### Facebook Insights
1. Access through your page's "Insights" tab
2. Monitor:
   - Page likes and reach
   - Post engagement
   - Follower demographics
   - Video views and engagement
