## Company Overview

🚢 **GK and A LOGISTICS SERVICES LTD**
**Building Nigeria's Future in Inland Ports & Logistics**

GK and A Logistics Services Ltd is a Nigerian-based maritime infrastructure and logistics development company committed to reshaping how goods move across the country.

Our mission is to provide world-class logistics solutions through the design, development, and operation of inland ports and related infrastructure that are efficient, sustainable, and economically impactful.

We are currently delivering two key projects that position us at the forefront of Nigeria's inland port evolution—both located in the strategically significant Ikorodu region of Lagos.

## Key Projects

### 📦 PROJECT ONE: 20,000 SQM NPA Lighter Terminal, Ikorodu
**Poised for Immediate Operations**

GK&A has secured a 20,000 square meter lease from the Nigerian Ports Authority (NPA) within the Lighter Terminal at Ikorodu. The project is operational and serves as a logistics hub to handle immediate demand and support the upcoming regional port development.

**Key Facilities:**
- 🏗 EPT Terminal for Empty Container Processing
- 🚛 Holding Bay for Truck Staging and Cargo Movement
- 📦 Logistics Services including Cargo Handling & Temporary Storage
- 🔁 Operational integration with ongoing port activities

**Strategic Objectives:**
- Reduce congestion and turnaround time in Lagos' port ecosystem
- Serve as a transitional platform for future large-scale port activity
- Create jobs and logistics stability in the Ikorodu axis
- Provide a platform for immediate business operations for private partners

### ⚓ PROJECT TWO: Ikorodu Regional Inland Port
**150 Hectares Underway – Expandable to 667 Hectares**

Located adjacent to the NPA Lighter Terminal, this project represents a bold vision for Nigeria's next major port hub. We are developing a Regional Inland Port on 150 hectares of land (with an expansion potential of up to 667 hectares), offering a long-term solution to the congestion issues at Apapa and Tin Can Island ports.

**Current Status:**
- Environmental & regulatory approvals secured
- Reclamation of the 150 hectares has commenced
- Approved for expansion up to 667 hectares
- Government support and technical groundwork in place

**Port Vision Includes:**
- 🛳 Full Port Terminal Infrastructure (cargo, container, RoRo)
- 🏭 Industrial Cluster Zones for logistics, finance, and innovation
- 📦 Warehousing and Value-Added Services
- 💡 Digital Innovation & Equipment Manufacturing Hubs

**Strategic Impact:**
- Decongest Lagos' current port bottlenecks
- Catalyze regional commerce and industrialization
- Attract long-term investments and PPP partnerships

## FAQ Section (Accordion Content)

### About Our Services
**What services does GK & A Logistics provide?**
We provide comprehensive maritime logistics services including port terminal operations, cargo handling, secure storage solutions, import/export coordination, and customs clearance support. Our services span both our operational NPA Lighter Terminal facility and our upcoming Ikorodu Regional Inland Port.

**What makes your facility unique?**
Our strategic location at NPA Lighter Terminal, Ikorodu provides direct access to maritime shipping routes and connectivity to Lagos' extensive road network. Our facility meets international standards with comprehensive security infrastructure. Additionally, our upcoming Ikorodu Regional Inland Port represents a bold vision for Nigeria's next major port hub.

**Do you handle both import and export cargo?**
Yes, we provide full-service logistics support for both import and export operations, including documentation, customs clearance assistance, and last-mile delivery coordination.

### Facility & Operations
**What is the capacity of your terminal facility?**
Our current facility features a 1,232.2m quay, 37,305.84m² apron area, 6,040.83m² climate-controlled warehouse, and 8,871.10m² paved stacking area, allowing us to handle multiple vessels and diverse cargo types simultaneously.

**How secure is your facility?**
We maintain comprehensive security infrastructure including 784.85m of perimeter fencing, multiple access control gates, and round-the-clock monitoring systems. All security measures comply with international standards.

**What types of cargo do you handle?**
We handle all types of maritime cargo including containerized goods, bulk cargo, oversized equipment, and specialized cargo requiring climate-controlled storage.

### Partnerships & Business
**How can we partner with GK & A Logistics?**
We welcome partnerships with logistics companies, shipping lines, freight forwarders, and businesses engaged in import/export activities. With our two key projects - the operational NPA Lighter Terminal and the upcoming Ikorodu Regional Inland Port - we offer multiple partnership opportunities. Contact us to discuss collaboration opportunities.

**Do you offer customized logistics solutions?**
Yes, we work closely with clients to develop tailored logistics solutions that meet their specific operational requirements and business objectives, whether for our current facility or future services at our Regional Inland Port.

**What are your operating hours?**
Our facility operates 24/7 to accommodate various shipping schedules and cargo handling needs. Contact us for specific scheduling requirements.

### Project-Specific Questions
**What is the status of your Ikorodu Regional Inland Port project?**
Our Ikorodu Regional Inland Port project is currently underway. We have secured environmental and regulatory approvals, and reclamation of the initial 150 hectares has commenced. The project is approved for expansion up to 667 hectares with government support and technical groundwork in place.

**How does the NPA Lighter Terminal project support the larger regional port development?**
Our 20,000 SQM NPA Lighter Terminal serves as a logistics hub to handle immediate demand and support the upcoming regional port development. It acts as a transitional platform for future large-scale port activity while providing immediate business operations for private partners.

**What services will be available at the Ikorodu Regional Inland Port?**
The Ikorodu Regional Inland Port will feature full port terminal infrastructure (cargo, container, RoRo), industrial cluster zones for logistics, finance, and innovation, warehousing and value-added services, and digital innovation & equipment manufacturing hubs.

### Technical & Compliance
**What standards does your facility comply with?**
Our facility complies with international standards including BS EN structural design standards, ISO specifications for materials and coatings, and Nigerian Building Code requirements.

**How do you ensure quality in your operations?**
We maintain rigorous quality assurance processes including regular testing, inspections, and certifications. Our operations are designed to meet international maritime logistics standards.

**What documentation do you provide?**
We provide comprehensive documentation including bills of lading, cargo manifests, customs clearance documents, and detailed operational reports as required.

## Technical Requirements

### Frontend Technology
- **Framework**: Next.js 15+ with React 19
- **Language**: TypeScript
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **UI Components**: ShadCN UI library
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach

### Backend Technology
- **Runtime**: Node.js 18+
- **Framework**: Express.js or Next.js API routes
- **Database**: PostgreSQL
- **ORM**: Prisma
- **Authentication**: JWT-based system

### Third-Party Integrations
- **Google Maps API**: For location services
- **Email Service**: SendGrid for notifications
- **SMS Gateway**: Twilio for alerts
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics
- **Search**: Elasticsearch or Algolia

### Hosting & Deployment
- **Frontend**: Vercel
- **Backend**: AWS or Railway
- **Database**: AWS RDS or PlanetScale
- **CDN**: Cloudflare or AWS CloudFront

### Performance Requirements
- Page load time: < 3 seconds
- API response time: < 500ms
- Mobile responsiveness: 100%
- Accessibility compliance: WCAG 2.1 AA
