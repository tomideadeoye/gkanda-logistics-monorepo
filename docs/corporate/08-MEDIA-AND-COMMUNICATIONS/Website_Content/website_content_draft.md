### Port Operations
**Hero Description:**
GK and A Logistics Services Ltd operates a state-of-the-art port terminal facility at the NPA Lighter Terminal, Ikorodu, providing comprehensive maritime logistics solutions that connect Nigerian businesses to global markets.

**Detailed Overview:**
Our port terminal facility features a 1,232.2-meter quay with 37,305.84 m² apron area, designed to accommodate multiple vessels simultaneously. This extensive waterfront infrastructure supports efficient cargo handling operations with deep foundation work, reinforced concrete structures, and marine-grade materials built to withstand saltwater exposure.

**Key Features:**
- **Multiple Vessel Berthing:** Our substantial quay length allows for simultaneous operations with several vessels, maximizing throughput capacity
- **Strategic Location:** Direct access to international maritime shipping routes with seamless connectivity to Lagos' extensive road network
- **Modern Infrastructure:** Deep foundation work, reinforced concrete structures, and marine-grade materials for reliable operations
- **Comprehensive Security:** 784.85m perimeter fencing with advanced security systems and controlled access points

**Benefits:**
