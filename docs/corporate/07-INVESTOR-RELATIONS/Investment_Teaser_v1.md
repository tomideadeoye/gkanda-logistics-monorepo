# CORPORATE PROFILE: GK&A LOGISTICS 
## IKORODU REGIONAL INLAND PORT & CUSTOMS DIRECT SHIP CALL TERMINAL

---

### **EXECUTIVE OVERVIEW**

GK&A Logistics is a premier infrastructure and terminal-operating group developing the **Ikorodu Regional Inland Port**. Located at the Ipakodo/Ebute waterfront on the Lagos Lagoon, this facility is the critical multimodal alternative to the hyper-congested Apapa and Tin Can Island ports.

Operating under a Public-Private Partnership (PPP) framework with the **Nigerian Inland Waterways Authority (NIWA)** and in collaboration with the **Sealink Consortium**, our terminal serves as a cornerstone of the **Customs Direct Ship Call** initiative. This allows seagoing vessels and barges to bypass traditional Atlantic-facing bottlenecks, delivering cargo directly into one of Nigeria's most robust industrial corridors.

---

### **KEY OPERATIONAL METRICS**

Our value proposition is defined by verified, institutional-grade infrastructure:

*   **Total Facility Footprint**: **~20.3 Hectares** (203,351 SQM) of continuous port and buffer zone development.
*   **Active Mixed-Cargo Hub**: **36,000 SQM** dedicated to containers, jumbo bags, bulk cargo processing, and reefer operations.
*   **Strategic Capacity**: **10,000 TEU** annual throughput with simultaneous multi-vessel berthing capabilities.
*   **Marine Infrastructure**: **540m Quay Wall** complemented by a **320m Quay Apron**.
*   **Enclosed Storage**: **4,460 SQM** dedicated general cargo warehousing.
*   **Asset Readiness**: High-load quay walls, twin-crane vessel support, and dedicated heavy-duty power generating units.

### **TECHNOLOGY & COLD CHAIN INFRASTRUCTURE**

GK&A is not just deploying physical concrete; we are deploying a fully integrated, digitally advanced smart terminal targeting a Q2/Q3 2026 Go-Live:

*   **Advanced Reefer Capability**: Comprehensive cold-chain infrastructure featuring reefer power racks and real-time telemetry (monitoring temperature, setpoints, power, and door events) routed directly into our Terminal Operating System (TOS).
*   **Automated Gate & Appointment Systems**: Traffic is governed by OCR and ANPR cameras, a digital truck appointment booking portal, and SMS notifications to eliminate queuing externalities.
*   **Warehouse Management (WMS)**: Digital batch/lot tracking with FEFO/FIFO rules natively integrating our jumbo-bag and bulk locations.
*   **Seamless API Integrations**: Deep technological integration with the **Nigerian Customs Single-Window API**, leading shipping lines, trucking partners, and the Port Authority.

### **COMPETITIVE MOATS**

GK&A holds structural and regulatory advantages designed for market dominance:

1.  **Section 14 CEMA Approval (Customs Direct Ship Call)**:
    Unlike standard bonded terminals, the Ikorodu Port is approved for **Direct Ship Call**. This eliminates secondary clearance delays, providing a "Fast-Track" logistics corridor for high-value industrial and agricultural cargo.

2.  **Multimodal Barge Connectivity**:
    The terminal leverages its 540m quay wall and the Lagos Lagoon to provide a seamless **Lagoon-to-Ocean** link. This reduces reliance on the Nigerian road network, cutting logistics costs by up to 25% and reducing the risk of cargo damage.

3.  **Integrated Traffic Infrastructure**:
    The facility features a private, high-capacity truck holding bay synchronized with our automated e-call-up system. This "buffer zone" ensures that terminal operations are never throttled by external congestion.

4.  **First-Mover Advantage**:
    Situated within 2km of major industrial giants, GK&A is the first private operator to secure full regulatory approval and commence operations in the Ikorodu inland port corridor. We are the natural gateway for raw materials and manufactured exports.
